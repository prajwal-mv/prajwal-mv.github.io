Project Technical Information

AirBnB Booking Analysis

Project Type : EDA Project

Tools : : Excel, Python, Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, MatplotLib, Github.

Project Demo : Github-link

Project Summary

Introduction:

Airbnb business model is an aggregator business model just like Uber and OYO. They act as a platform for hosts and travellers. Hosts can put up their property for stay, and if someone wishes to stay there, the hosts get rent in exchange. For attracting people Host prefers to use different words to describe different room types such as private room, entire home/apt, shared room. Our experiment can help understand what the reason for the classification of such labels by data analysis could be. Our Project is to Investigate patterns and explanations in the context and convey the results in a dynamic and visual manner.

Problem Statement:

The problem that this case study is dealing with predicts the location that a user is most likely to book for the first time. The accurate prediction helps to decrease the average time required to book by sharing more personalized recommendations and also in better forecasting of the demand. We use the browser’s session data as well as the user’s demographic information that is provided to us to create features that help in solving the problem.

Approach:

To perform Exploratory Data Analysis on AIRBNB, we imported Python libraries like Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib and Seaborn to display the analysis of AIRBNB dataset in Graphical form through BarPlot, Histogram and Heatmap etc. Statistical graphics and other Data visualization methods are used to summarize their main characteristics of “AIRBNB dataset”.

Conclusion:

1)Private room has lowest minimum nights avg, and cheaper compared to Entire room, and its prices are very similar to Share rooms when charges are compared on per night basis. 2)Private rooms are the most reviewed across NYC 3)Brooklyn has Cheaper per night charges for private rooms compared to other boroughs, 3 of the most booked neighborhoods are from Brooklyn, Williamsburg, Bushwick, Bedford-Stuyvesant. 4)Manhattan is the hub of the center of activities like Central park, times square and has the busiest subway, 5)Entire home provides more space, Entire home Charges in Manhattan are very close to Private room.
